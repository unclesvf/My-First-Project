# Voice Characters for History Lessons

This document details the narrator character properties for each of the 51 lessons, organized for voice selection in Zonos TTS.

## Voice Requirements Summary

| Gender | Count | Age Range |
|--------|-------|-----------|
| Male | 30 | 13-18 years |
| Female | 20 | 12-17 years |
| Adult Male | 1 | Adult (Theodore Roosevelt) |

## Recommended Reference Audio Sources

1. **For teenage voices:** LibriTTS audiobook recordings, VCTK dataset
2. **For accented voices:** Mozilla Common Voice (multi-language), CREMA-D (diverse ethnicities)
3. **For African American voices:** CREMA-D dataset includes diverse speakers
4. **For immigrant accents:** Consider voice actors or ethnic community recordings
5. **For historical authenticity:** Period drama audiobooks often have appropriate voice styles
