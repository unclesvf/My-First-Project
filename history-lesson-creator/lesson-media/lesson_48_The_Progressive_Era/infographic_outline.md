## Key Vocabulary

- **Progressive Era:** Period (1900-1920) of political and social reform movements.
- **Muckrakers:** Journalists exposing corruption and social problems.
- **Upton Sinclair:** Author of 'The Jungle' exposing meatpacking industry horrors.
- **Theodore Roosevelt:** Progressive president who 'trust-busted' monopolies and conserved nature.
- **Hull House:** Settlement house founded by Jane Addams to help immigrants and poor.
- **Seventeenth Amendment:** 1913 amendment providing direct election of senators.
