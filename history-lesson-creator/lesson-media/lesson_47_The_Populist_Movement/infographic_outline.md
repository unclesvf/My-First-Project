# Infographic Outline: The Populist Movement

**Subtitle:** Farmers Organize (1890s)

**Time Period:** 1890s

## Sections

### 1. Drowning in Debt
I'm Emma, sixteen years old, and I live on a wheat farm in Kansas. My family works from sunrise to sunset planting, tending, and harvesting wheat. We work harder every year.

### 2. The Farmers' Alliance
Farmers across the country are organizing. The Farmers' Alliance starts in Texas and spreads across the South and West. By 1890, it has over a million members.

### 3. The People's Party

## Key Vocabulary

- **Populist Party:** People's Party formed by farmers demanding economic reforms.
- **William Jennings Bryan:** Populist Democrat famous for 'Cross of Gold' speech.
- **Cross of Gold Speech:** Bryan's 1896 speech supporting free silver coinage.
- **Free Silver:** Populist demand to coin unlimited silver, increasing money supply.
- **Farmers' Alliance:** Organization of farmers fighting railroad and bank monopolies.
