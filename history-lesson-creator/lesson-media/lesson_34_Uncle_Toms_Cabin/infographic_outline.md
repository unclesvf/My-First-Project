## Key Vocabulary

- **Uncle Tom's Cabin:** 1852 anti-slavery novel by Harriet Beecher Stowe that influenced Northern opinion.
- **Harriet Beecher Stowe:** Author whose novel Uncle Tom's Cabin galvanized Northern opposition to slavery.
- **Uncle Tom:** Main character in Stowe's novel—faithful slave sold away from family.
- **Simon Legree:** Cruel slave owner in novel who became symbol of slavery's brutality.
- **Eliza:** Character who fled slavery by crossing icy Ohio River with her baby.
- **Abolitionist Literature:** Books and publications advocating end of slavery.
