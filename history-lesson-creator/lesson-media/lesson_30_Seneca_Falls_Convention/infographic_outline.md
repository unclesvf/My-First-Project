## Key Vocabulary

- **Seneca Falls Convention:** 1848 meeting launching organized women's rights movement.
- **Declaration of Sentiments:** Document declaring men and women created equal, modeled on Declaration of Independence.
- **Elizabeth Cady Stanton:** Key organizer of Seneca Falls and lifelong women's rights advocate.
- **Lucretia Mott:** Quaker abolitionist who helped organize Seneca Falls.
- **Women's Suffrage:** Right of women to vote—most controversial Seneca Falls demand.
- **Separate Spheres:** Ideology that men belong in public life, women in domestic life.
