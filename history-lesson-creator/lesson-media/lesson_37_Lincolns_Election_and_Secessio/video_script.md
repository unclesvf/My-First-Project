## Chapter 3: The Confederacy Forms
*Duration: 1-2 minutes*

**Narration:**
South Carolina's secession triggers a chain reaction. Within weeks, six more states of the Deep South follow: Mississippi, Florida, Alabama, Georgia, Louisiana, and Texas. All cite Lincoln's election and Northern hostility to slavery as reasons for leaving. In February 1861, delegates from the seceded states meet in Montgomery, Alabama.
