## Key Vocabulary

- **Abraham Lincoln:** First Republican president whose election triggered Southern secession.
- **Secession:** Southern states leaving the Union to form the Confederacy.
- **Confederate States of America:** Nation formed by eleven seceding Southern states.
- **Jefferson Davis:** President of the Confederate States of America.
- **Fort Sumter:** Federal fort in Charleston where Civil War began with Confederate attack.
- **Border States:** Slave states (Delaware, Maryland, Kentucky, Missouri) that stayed in Union.
