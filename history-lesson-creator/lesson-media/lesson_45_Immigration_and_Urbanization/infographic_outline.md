### 5. The New Colossus
By 1900, I'm twenty-two. I've lived in America eight years. I work as a skilled shoemaker now, earning better wages.

## Key Vocabulary

- **Ellis Island:** Immigration station in New York Harbor where millions entered America.
- **New Immigration:** Late 1800s wave of immigrants from Southern and Eastern Europe.
- **Tenement:** Crowded, often unsafe urban apartment building housing poor immigrants.
- **Urbanization:** Growth of cities as people moved from farms to urban areas.
- **Statue of Liberty:** Symbol of American welcome to immigrants, gifted by France.
- **Steerage:** Cheapest, most crowded section of ship used by poor immigrants.
