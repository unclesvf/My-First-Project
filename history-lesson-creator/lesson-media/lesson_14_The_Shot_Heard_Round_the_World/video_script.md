## Chapter 1: The Midnight Ride
*Duration: 1-2 minutes*

**Narration:**
My name is Nathan, and I am seventeen years old. I'm a Minuteman—part of our militia trained to be ready at a minute's notice. Tonight, April 18, 1775, that training will be tested. Word has reached us that British troops are marching from Boston to seize our weapons and gunpowder stored in Concord, and possibly to arrest Samuel Adams and John Hancock who are hiding in Lexington.
