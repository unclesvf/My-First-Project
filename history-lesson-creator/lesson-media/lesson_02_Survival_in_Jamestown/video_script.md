## Chapter 4: The First Representative Government
*Duration: 1-2 minutes*

**Narration:**
In 1619, something remarkable happened. The Virginia Company allowed us to form the House of Burgesses, the first representative assembly in the American colonies. We could elect burgesses to make local laws, as long as they didn't contradict the company's rules or English law. For the first time, colonists had a voice in their own government.
