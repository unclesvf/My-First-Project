## Key Vocabulary

- **Women's Suffrage:** The right of women to vote, achieved nationally in 1920.
- **Nineteenth Amendment:** 1920 amendment granting women the right to vote.
- **Susan B. Anthony:** Leading suffragist who fought for decades for women's voting rights.
- **Elizabeth Cady Stanton:** Suffragist leader from Seneca Falls through late 1800s.
- **Alice Paul:** Militant suffragist who picketed White House and led hunger strikes.
- **Carrie Chapman Catt:** Suffragist leader whose state-by-state strategy helped win amendment.
