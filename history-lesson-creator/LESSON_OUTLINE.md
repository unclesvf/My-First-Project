## Content Structure for Each Lesson

Each lesson includes:
- **Story**: 3-5 chapters from a first-person narrator (age-appropriate perspective)
- **Flashcards**: 6-8 key vocabulary terms with definitions
- **Quiz**: 6-8 multiple-choice questions with explanations

**Narrator Examples**:
- Young colonists, soldiers, enslaved persons (sensitively portrayed)
- Immigrants, factory workers, reformers
- Children of historical figures
- Ordinary people experiencing extraordinary events

**7th Grade Appropriate**:
- Age-appropriate language and themes
- Balanced perspectives on controversial topics
- Emphasis on cause and effect
- Connection to modern civics and citizenship
